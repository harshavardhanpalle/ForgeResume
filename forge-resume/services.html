How scoring works

Three signals, one score

Keyword match

Forge extracts the skills and tools named in the job description and checks which ones appear in your resume — and where.

Structure check

Missing dates, unclear job titles, or sections out of expected order all reduce how reliably an ATS can parse your resume.

Impact density

Forge counts how many of your bullet points include a measurable result, and flags the ones that don't.
